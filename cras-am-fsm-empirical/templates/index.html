Welcome to Empirical, hosted by the Reberlab

This is a site for coordinating online psychology experiments. If you are interested in participating, please contact reberlab@gmail.com.

Once cleared, ads for experiments open for recruiting will go here. You can also look for opportunities to participate in paid research through Amazon's Mechanical Turk system.

Privacy and confidentiality statement
In our research, we maintain a strong commitment to responsible conduct of research and ethical practice. All of our data are de-identified and stored securely. During ongoing research, data are only available to authorized personnel. All research personnel maintain up to date training and research ethics certification. Our research often leads to publication in peer-reviewed journals. Data presented, described or made available through that process are carefully fully anonymized to avoid compromising the privacy of our participants.
